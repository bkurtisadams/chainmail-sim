Optional Rules

Charge impetus bonus, prisoners step, and cavalry-charge morale check will appear here as their slices land. Chainmail p.16–18.

Movement (p.9–11)

Each unit's distance moved this turn is tracked against its type's allowance (the drag ruler and the unit pane show it). Off (default) is advisory: you can move freely and the sim only warns when a unit exceeds its allowance, so freeform placement still works. On caps each unit's movement at the rulebook limit. Resets every round.
